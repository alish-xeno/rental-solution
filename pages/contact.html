Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for rooms?

Go to the "Find Rooms" page and use our filters to search by location, price, and room type.

Is there any fee for students?

No, our platform is completely free for students. We don't charge any broker fees.

Are all listings verified?

Yes, we verify all property listings with legal documents before publishing them on our platform.

How can landlords list their properties?

Landlords can register through our "Register" page and submit their property details for verification.

What if I face issues with a landlord?

Contact our support team immediately, and we'll help mediate and resolve the issue.
