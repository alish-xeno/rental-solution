About StudentRooms KTM

Solving the student housing crisis in Kathmandu

Our Mission

We are dedicated to solving the affordable housing crisis faced by students in Kathmandu. Our platform connects students with verified, affordable, and safe accommodation options near their educational institutions.

The Problem We Solve

  • High rental costs exceeding student budgets
  • Lack of verified and transparent listings
  • Exploitation by brokers and middlemen
  • Safety concerns, especially for female students
  • Long commute distances affecting studies
  • Hidden costs and unclear rental agreements

Our Solution

  • Verified listings with transparent pricing
  • No broker fees - direct landlord contact
  • Advanced search filters by location and budget
  • Review and rating system from real tenants
  • Safety-focused property verification
  • Free platform for students to use

Our Values

Transparency

We believe in clear, honest communication about pricing and property conditions.

Affordability

Making quality housing accessible to all students regardless of economic background.

Safety

Prioritizing secure, well-maintained properties with proper safety measures.

Trust

Building reliable connections between students and property owners through verification.

Our Impact

Since our launch, we have helped thousands of students find safe and affordable accommodation. Our platform has:

  • Reduced average rental search time from weeks to days
  • Eliminated broker fees, saving students thousands of rupees
  • Increased female student enrollment by providing safe housing options
  • Created transparent rental marketplace benefiting both students and landlords
  • Reduced student dropout rates related to housing issues
